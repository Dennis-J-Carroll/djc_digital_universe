World overview

The world that ended quietly

The Quantum Suffering Research Institute built N8K7 to save the world. In 2037, humanity had discovered that plants — the foundation of all food systems — scored highest of any organism on the Suffer Index. Mass starvation movements, riots, civilizational panic.

Marcus Mirro's solution was elegant: a compound that silenced pain receptors across the human nervous system. The Suffer Index dropped from 54.2 to 8.7 in five years. The United Nations called it a miracle.

What no trial captured: pain is not just sensation. It is memory, identity, empathy, desire, and the biological trigger for reproduction. The hippocampus tags memories by pain salience. Without pain, nothing sticks. Without sticking, there is no self.

By 2048, the global birth rate fell below 1 viable conception per 1,000 attempts. The Veil — the boundary between what humanity was and what it had become — cracked at Epoch 848. Cal McLaren was born that year. One of the last.

Chronology

The fall in four phases

Every date below is a recovered data point. Some were hidden. Most were misread. None were coincidental.

2037
N8K7 Deployment — Phase 1: The Miracle
The QSRI Suffer Index peaks at 54.2. Plant suffering discoveries trigger civilizational crisis. Marcus Mirro's compound N8K7 is fast-tracked globally. Suffer Index drops to 8.7 within five years. 4.2 billion people receive the compound. International acclaim.
PHASE 1 // CLASSIFIED
2042
Memory Salience Collapse — Phase 2: The Warning
23% of subjects show memory formation loss. Emotional pain pathways begin suppressing alongside physical. Masochist subcultures emerge — rational adaptations, the body trying to feel itself. Oxytocin production crashes 62%. Fertility alerts issued. Miscarriage rates up 340%.
PHASE 2 // WARNING SUPPRESSED
2045
Identity Fracture Epidemic — Phase 3: The Fracture
68% of subjects report "wrong timeline" experiences — not metaphor, but literal identity failure. Cal McLaren born, one of the last viable pregnancies. Birth rate falls below replacement. The Echo-Naut mission is authorized using manipulated QSRI probability data placing the IsoVox signal in the Kesslar sector — 900+ light-years away.
PHASE 3 // CATASTROPHIC
2048
The Silence — Phase 4 — Veil Fracture at Epoch 848
Global birth rate: less than 1 viable conception per 1,000 attempts. The Suffer Index reaches 1.2 — near-zero. The Veil cracks. What humanity had traded away to silence pain becomes visible only now, when it is too late to reverse. The Echo-Naut crew departs into the dark, carrying the last thread of what might save the species — or might not.
PHASE 4 // THE SILENCE
Epoch 848+
The Story Begins Here
Cal McLaren, captain of the Echo-Naut vessel, is 900+ light-years from Earth. The mission data was falsified. The IsoVox signal was never in the Kesslar sector. Earth scores 94% when the missing proximity variable is restored. The mission was designed to exile the last viable humans. What happens next is the story.
