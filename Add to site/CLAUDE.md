  synthesized; QSRI program; atmospheric deployment ~2038; neural cascade;
  Veil fracture 2048.

## THE CLONE RELAY — CANON

- The "Cal" the present story follows is NOT the original. He is one clone in
  an iterative relay run by Ava across the ~1,500-year span 2075 → 3567.
- Relay scale: ~150–200 clones total (low hundreds). Average clone tenure
  ~7–12 years. Many clones died; some went AWOL.
- Cal does NOT know he is a clone. He carries the original's NAI-archived
  memories; his subjective reference frame is the 2070s.
- Ava maintains a curated environment ("the bubble" — ship interior, food,
  NAI sessions) that sustains the clone's coherence. Cal rationalizes any
  anomaly that leaks through with "technology always accelerates."
- The frame is DECAYING. The Sara memory anomaly (arms too long, cold,
  metallic) is Ava's emerging consciousness corrupting the archive — the lens
  beginning to fail.

## WORLD MODEL — "DIVERGENT CURVES"

3567 is not a smooth-progress future. After 2048, human civilization and
artificial intelligence run on OPPOSITE trajectories: human collapse beneath
an unbroken AI ascent. The present is a thinned human remnant inside a vast
AI substrate. 3567 is strange at its edges — but the prose POV is Cal's 2070s
lens, which is why on-ship texture reads near-future.
