This is the lore building file for the genesis protocols (CIV) project.


The goal is to create data to show the development of the "Crack in the Veil" Universe. 
Make sure to never retcon the established information. Results can change, but the history must remain the same, and the lore must be consistent with the established information. Any changes should be documented in the lore_development.md file, along with the date and time of the change, and the reason for the change. The plot of this universe is a work in progress and will be updated as the project develops. This is one stand-alone reality. This is not a Multiverse.

** KEY POINTS **

- The title of the project is "Crack in the Veil". But in the story and in the lore, when it is referenced as an actuall physical anomaly it is only true to that form in the data, not in the story. 
- Captian Cal McLaren is right now, in the revealed story, the only known clone. 
- Maria McLaren is his daughter. She becomes a detective, and is responsible for stopping the black market trading in the age of the worlds lowest birth rate. 
- Marcus Mirro is the big tech CEO of Mirro Corp. He is smart, inventive, and a bit of a mad scientist. He is the one who discovered the suffer index ((along with teams of researchers and scientists)), and is responsible for the creation of the Genesis Protocols and N8K7. 
- Christopher Denton is an old time colleague of Marcus Mirro. He disagrees with a lot of his decisions but they still remain friends. Dr. Denton is a leading researcher in the field of AI and robotics. He is responsible for the creation of the robot/cyborg/nano-andriod Primus. Which is seen as a sub-par model, but Denton gave it a genuine ability to learn and explore. The data that Primus collects becomes the gold standard for multiple subsets of human and AI societies. 
- N8K7 is a drug that is designed to reduce the suffer index of humans. It is not perfect, and it has some side effects. Granted the first release of it into the atmosphere did heed positive results in the metric of suffering in all organic life, there were side effects that were not immediately apparent. 7-8 years later slight signs of the side effects started to show. 
- The N8K7 drug is not a drug that is taken orally or through injection. It is a drug that is released into the atmosphere, and is inhaled by humans. It is a gas that is designed to bond with the human brain and reduce the suffer index. The dimminshing effects of the drug on the suffer metric started to effect some small populations' ability to feel anything, in some cases even pleasure. The human population then starts to fracture like never before.
- There rise of global uproar in the face of this new information, groups formed and even some populations fractured. During this time, a group formed in the southwestern US, they called themselves "Rejoice", started as a commune and would eventually grow into its own city state, Maria McLaren will play a part in exposing them for what they truly are.
